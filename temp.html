What Is the Classic Physique Division?

TThe Classic Physique division, introduced in 2016, aims to revive the aesthetics of bodybuilding's Golden Era, emphasizing symmetry, proportion, and overall balance over sheer muscle mass. Competitors are judged on their ability to present a harmonious and aesthetically pleasing physique, reminiscent of legends like Arnold Schwarzenegger and Frank Zane. Mandatory poses include traditional stances such as the front double biceps and side chest, allowing athletes to showcase their physique's flow and artistry.

How It Differs from Other Divisions:

  • Height & Weight Limits: Specific caps based on height (e.g., 5'7" = 180 lbs).
  • Emphasis on Aesthetics: Rewards V-tapers, small waists, and balanced development.
  • Posing Style: Includes a unique "Classic Pose" inspired by iconic physiques.
